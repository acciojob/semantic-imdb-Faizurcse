it is a comedy-drama film based on the novel "Five Point Someone" by Chetan Bhagat. The story revolves around three friends - Rancho (Aamir Khan), Farhan (R. Madhavan), and Raju (Sharman Joshi) - who embark on a quest to find their lost friend Rancho. The narrative unfolds through a series of flashbacks, showcasing their journey through engineering college, their unique bond, and their encounters with the educational system.
Aamir Khan played a pivotal role in the film "3 Idiots," contributing significantly to its success. In the movie, Aamir portrayed the character Ranchhoddas "Rancho" Shamaldas Chanchad, a free-spirited and unconventional engineering student. Rancho is portrayed as a genius who questions the traditional and often oppressive educational system, urging his friends to pursue their passions rather than succumb to societal and parental expectations.
The film addresses societal expectations, the pressure on students in the education system, and the pursuit of passion over conventional success. Aamir Khan's character, Rancho, becomes an emblematic figure encouraging unconventional thinking and challenging the norms of the traditional education system.
